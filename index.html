About Us

Rainfall Studios is an innovative and creative game development studio...

We are making PC Games with an independent soul.

Rainfall Studios is an innovative and creative game development studio that aims to fill the gaps in the industry. We are currently working on a game called ‘Sails Out’, has a completely unique structure and aims to offer an unprecedented experience in the market. This game offers players a real-time ship captaining experience, invites them to explore the high seas and rule different cities. Players will also have the opportunity to test their strategic skills by participating in fascinating country battles.
